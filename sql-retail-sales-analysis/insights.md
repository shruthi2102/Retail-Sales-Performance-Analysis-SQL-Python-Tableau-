# Retail Sales Analysis – Key Business Insights

## 1. Electronics Drive Most Revenue
Products in the Electronics category, such as laptops and monitors, generate the largest portion of revenue due to higher price points.

## 2. Laptop is the Highest Revenue Product
Among all products, laptops contribute the most revenue, indicating strong demand for high-value items.

## 3. Revenue is Concentrated Among Top Customers
A small group of customers contributes a significant share of total revenue, highlighting the importance of customer retention.

## 4. Repeat Customers Provide Consistent Sales
Customers who place multiple orders generate steady revenue and are key to long-term business growth.

## 5. Geographic Sales Insights
Certain cities contribute more to overall revenue, suggesting opportunities for targeted regional marketing strategies.

## 6. Sales Show Monthly Trends
Revenue analysis over time reveals trends that can help businesses plan inventory and marketing campaigns.

## 7. Product Category Performance
Accessories generate frequent orders while Electronics generate higher revenue per order.
